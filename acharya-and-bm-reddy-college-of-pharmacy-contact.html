Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy Bangalore Contact Details

April 6, 2021

Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy in one of the premier pharmacy institutions in India. The college has received several awards over the years and it also has the distinction of being the first of the Acharya institutes to be awarded the SIRO status. The college is also one of the best industry linked pharmacy colleges in India.

Students interested in studying pharmacy at Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy will need to contact the college to get information about admissions.

The college’s postal address is Acharya Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan Road, Acharya PO, Soldevanahalli, Bangalore-560 107, India.

Students can contact the college through email at admissions@acharya.ac.in and call at +917406644449 for information about admission or the details of the courses offered. Students can also send a message to the WhatsApp number +18147054141 for any information.

Please browse through the college’s official website for more information about Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy courses.
