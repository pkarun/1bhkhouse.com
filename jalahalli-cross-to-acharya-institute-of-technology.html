How to Reach from Jalahalli Cross to Acharya Institute of Technology

April 7, 2021

Acharya Institute of Technology is one of the premier institutes for engineering and related courses in Bangalore. Many students are looking for ways to get from Jalahalli Cross to Acharya Institute of Technology. Here is a quick look at the distance, ways to reach, and the time it will take to get to Acharya Institute of Technology from Jalahalli Cross.

The distance between both locations is 8 KMs, and it takes almost 21 minutes to cover this distance in a car. The time taken for the journey can vary depending on the traffic and the driving conditions.

Public Transport from Jalahalli Cross to Acharya Institute of Technology

You can also choose to take a bus from Jalahalli Cross to Acharya Institute of Technology. The buses leave from Jalahalli Cross at regular intervals. You can take the 502HE that takes nearly half an hour to reach the Acharya Institute of Technology, and the bus ticket costs 17 rupees. You can also take 248BB, 248J, 248Z, and 502K. Cab Route from Jalahalli Cross to Acharya Institute of Technology

Another way to commute is to get an Ola cab to reach your destination. The prices for the cabs can change depending on the time of the day, cab availability, and the traffic. The cab can cost anywhere from 110 to 140 rupees.
