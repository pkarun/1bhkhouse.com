Acharya College of Engineering Placements

May 23, 2021

The placement cell at Acharya College of Engineering invites companies from all over India as well as abroad. The companies seek the most talented and capable employees for their workforce, and Acharya College of Engineering aims to provide them the best resources for their organizations.

With an excellent track record of placed students, the placement cell works hard to match students with the companies that will make the best use of their talents.

The placement cell also trains the students and works with them to provide them with an in-depth understanding of the hiring process.

Students can take mock tests and appear in group discussions to perform well in the placement interviews. If you are looking for more details on the Acharya College of Engineering Bangalore placements, then read on.

In 2019, a total of 70 companies visited Acharya Institute of Technology for placements. The highest package offered in 2019 was 11.4 LPA.

The top recruiting companies in Acharya College of Engineering were Infosys, TCS, IBM, TATA AIG, Bosch, and Tech Mahindra. The students must clear all exams with good marks to not face any issues in their placements.

In 2020, more than 80% of the students got placements in the engineering division. They were offered packages as high as 20 LPA, and the lowest package was 5-6 LPA. Students got recruited through campus placement and also got internships in various organizations.

If you are looking for more information on the Acharya College of Engineering placements, you can browse through https://www.acharya.ac.in/. You can also get in touch by sending the college an email at  admissions@acharya.ac.in or call them at +917406644449 for more information about admissions.
