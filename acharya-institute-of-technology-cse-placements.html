Acharya Institute of Technology Cse Placements

May 23, 2021

Acharya Institute is a premier education destination for students that want to increase their understanding and knowledge in their chosen stream. Computer Science and Engineering is one of the most popular courses at the Acharya Institute. The information about CSE placements is a query that most students have when they look at Acharya Institute for this course.

Acharya Institute CSE Placements in 2020

In the year 2020, the placements were in full drive for the CSE batch. Almost 85% of the students got placed and the highest salary package was 9 lakh per annum while the lower end was 4.5 lakh per annum. The companies that visited the college varied from TCS and Infosys to several start-up companies. Students can also apply for internships in companies depending on their chosen career stream. Companies also keep visiting the Acharya Institute through the year to look for talented students in different semesters.

The college also provides training to students on how to appear in interviews by conducting mock interviews and group discussion sessions. The students can hone their interview skills by attending the sessions given by the placement cell so that they can perform excellently at the actual interviews.

For more information about the Computer Science and Engineering course, please browse through https://www.acharya.ac.in/ait/computer-science-engineering.html. You can also send an email to  admissions@acharya.ac.in or call +917406644449 to get any information about the college or the courses you are interested in.
