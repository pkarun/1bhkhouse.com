Acharya Institute BBA Fee Structure

April 7, 2021

Acharya Institute is the first choice of students looking for higher studies after completing their 10+2. Students interested in BBA often want to know the fee structure for the BBA course.

The total fee for BBA at Acharya Institute is ₹250,000. This is a full-time course of three years duration, and you can pay the payment on a per-year basis. The breakdown of the fees is 90000 for the first year, 60000 for the second year, and 60000 for the third year along with administrative fees.

The average salary for a student completing this course is 4.2 LPA, and students can get jobs through campus placements where multinational organizations visit the campus.

Eligibility for BBA at Acharya Institute, Bangalore

BBA is one of the most coveted streams available at Acharya Institutes under the business management category. If you are interested in BBA, you need to have completed a two-year pre-university course in Karnataka State or equivalent to getting admission into this course.

Details about the BBA Course at Acharya Institute, Bangalore

This course takes into account the essential requirements of business organizations from their employees. While teaching students how to deal with the practical aspects of the various business fields, this course also helps students build a foundation for future study. The final semester of the course allows students to specialize in their chosen field.
