Acharya College MBA Course Details, Bangalore

April 6, 2021

Introduction

The MBA course offered by the prestigious Acharya College is a two year programme taught at Acharya Institute of Technology. Students that enrol in this course also have the option to choose International Immersion.

The MBA program is designed to be in sync with the newest developments in the field of business, considering which the program also offers an incubation program for budding entrepreneurs. The Acharya College is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University

If you are interested in enrolling for this course, here are the details that will help you to proceed further with the enrollment.

Course Details for MBA, Acharya College, Bangalore

The MBA course has a two-year duration. The course’s highlights are International MBA, a business incubator, Cambridge Business English Training, Pearson Certification Programs, Entrepreneurship training at EDII-Ahmedabad, dedicated training programs for business skills, and more.

You can find the complete details about the course syllabus by clicking this link.

Admission Process for MBA, Acharya College, Bangalore

There are 240 seats available for the MBA Course. Admission is based on MAT / KMAT / KPGCET / CMAT Scores.

Domestic admissions can be done on a walk-in basis where students can visit the campus and meet counselors. Counselors will check if the student is eligible for admission. Once the admission is offered, students can choose the payment mode and start the course once the session begins.

Students can seek admission through online/email by visiting https://admissions.acharya.ac.in/ and filling in the online application. Students can also send in admission requests to admissions@acharya.ac.in.

The counselors will get in touch with the students through phone or email. Once the students send scanned copies of the relevant documents to the counselor, the admission offer will be made if the students meet the eligibility requirements. The fee can be paid online or by visiting the college in person.

International Students Seeking Admission in MBA, Acharya College, Bangalore

International students need to register through FRRO within 14 days of their arrival in India. They can download the application form via the official website and submit the filled application form along with relevant documents to international@acharya.ac.in. Further formalities such as visa application, class commencement date, important documents to carry, will be informed to the students by the college administration.

Eligibility Criteria for MBA, Acharya College, Bangalore

Candidates need to have a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university. The minimum marks should be 50% in aggregate of all subjects. Admission also takes into account MAT / KMAT / KPGCET / CMAT Scores. Candidates who have a good track record in Co-Curricular and Extra-Curricular activities might get an advantage in the admission and selection process.

Important Exam Dates

For students that are planning to appear in the entrance exams, here are the registration dates for MAT / KMAT / KPGCET / CMAT.

MAT – 5 January’ 2021 – 1 March 2021 (Exam is held four times a year)

KMAT – January 2021 (Exam is held once a year)

KPGCET – 29 December 2020 – 1 Decembar 2021 (Exam is held once a year)

CMAT – 23 December 2020 – 31 January 2021 (Exam is held once a year)

Fees Details

The fee for the 2 year MBA course at Acharya College, Bangalore is 1 Lakh.
