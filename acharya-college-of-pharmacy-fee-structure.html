Acharya College Of Pharmacy, Bangalore – Fee Structure

April 7, 2021

The Acharya College of Pharmacy offers a range of courses for students that want to pursue higher studies in a recognized and award-winning institution.

The college offers four courses for pharmacy students. The Bachelor of Pharmacy is a 4-year course, while the Diploma in Pharmacy, Doctorate of Pharmacy, and Master of Pharmacy are each 2 years of duration.

If you are looking for the fee of each course, you can check out the fees mentioned in the table below.

Please keep in mind that the fee amounts can change as per the rules and regulations decided by the college. It is a good idea to get in touch with the administration to get the exact fee you will need to pay for each course.

Fee Structure Acharya College of Pharmacy, Bangalore

CourseDurationFees
Bachelor of Pharmacy [B.Pharm]4 year(s)6,69,000
Diploma in Pharmacy [D.Pharm]2 year(s)1,50,000
Doctorate of Pharmacy [Pharm.D]2 year(s)5,00,000
Master of Pharmacy [M.Pharm]2 year(s)3,50,000

About the Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy

The institution was established in 1992 by the Acharya Group, and it is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences.

The college is approved by AICTE and accredited with the NBA. By providing students with top-quality education in the field of pharmacy, the institute has won the distinction of being one of the top pharmacy colleges in Bangalore.

Students can choose from various disciplines for the course of their choices, such as pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmaceutical analysis, and more.
