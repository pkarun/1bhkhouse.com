Acharya Institute Of Technology Aeronautical Engineering Fee Structure

April 7, 2021

Aeronautical Engineering at Acharya Institute of Technology is one of the most valued degrees for students who are looking to make a mark in the world of engineering. Students are often looking for the fee structure of the Aeronautical Engineering course at Acharya Institute of Technology.

The fee for the four-year course is paid on a per-year basis, and the first-year fee includes the registration fee and miscellaneous charges.

The fee can also vary depending on the kind of hostel accommodation you choose. The B.E.’s total fee in Aeronautical Engineering at Acharya Institute of Technology can be up to 11 lacs or more.

Year Fee
1st year 5.56 lacs
2nd year 1.85 lakh
3rd year 1.85 lakh
4th year 1.85 lakh
