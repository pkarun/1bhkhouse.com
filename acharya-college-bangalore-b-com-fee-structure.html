Acharya College Bangalore B.com Fee Structure

May 23, 2021

The B.Com degree from Acharya College Bangalore is a good starting point for students who are looking to make a mark in the field of commerce. The Bachelor of Commerce is a three year course and the Department of Commerce has an intake capacity of 60 students.

Since the department was set up in 2009, it has been offering students excellent opportunities in the field of commerce whether it is for learning or gaining practical experience. The department also started a PG program in commerce in 2010 and in 2011 two more PG programs were added to the total list of programs provided at the Department of Commerce.

B.Com Fee Structure at the Acharya College Bangalore

The B.Com course has a three year duration and the total fee for the course is 1.68 lakhs. Please keep in mind that the total fees can change depending on various factors. It is a good idea to get in touch with the college administration before making your mind up about the course.

The eligibility for the B.Com course is as follows: pass in Pre-University / Higher Secondary / 10+2 / ‘A’ Level or equivalent of any recognized board or council in any discipline.

The candidates who have done a three year diploma in commercial practice in Karnataka can also get direct admission in 2nd year B.Com.

For more information about the course, please check out the course specific page at https://www.acharya.ac.in/aigs/bcom.html. You can also check out the Acharya Institute at www.acharya.ac.in. For admission related queries, please send an email to  admissions@acharya.ac.in or call +917406644449.
