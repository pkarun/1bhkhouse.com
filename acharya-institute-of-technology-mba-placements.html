Acharya Institute of Technology Mba Placements

May 23, 2021

The MBA course from Acharya Institute of Technology offers great prospects for students who are looking for jobs in multinational companies. The AIT MBA placements bring several big names to the campus so that they can find new talent and train the students for working in their companies.

In the past years, the MBA placements at Acharya Institute of Technology saw recruitment drives from companies such as Infosys, HSBC, ICICI, Huawei, Larsen and Turbo, Toyota, Mercedes Benz, Tata AIG, Kotak Mahindra Bank, HP, TCS, Wipro, and Bata.

More than 56% of the students got placed through campus placements. On-campus, the highest package offered was 10 LPA and lowest package offered was 3 LPA. Nearly 6.7% of students got internships in various companies depending on their skill level and company requirements.

The placement cell of the college also works hard to train and educate students about the various ways in which they can secure a job with a company. The placement cell conducts mock interviews and training sessions to give the students an insight into the hiring process.

In all the Acharya Institute of Technology offers ample opportunities for placements and students can make use of the facilities offered by the institute to further strengthen their options for getting hired.
