Acharya Institute MBA Fee Structure

May 23, 2021

The Acharya Institute of Technology is one of the first choices of students looking for an MBA in Bangalore and nearby areas. The institute offers a two-year Master of Business Administration program. Once the students complete this program, they can also go for international immersion.

Students interested in studying MBA at Acharya Institute need to know about the MBA fee structure to get a better idea of the feasibility of the course. As the system is a two-year duration with regular classes, the total fee breakdown for the MBA course at Acharya Institute is as follows.

Acharya Institute of Technology MBA Fee Structure

Total fee for the course MBA course can fall between 6.5 to 8 lakhs. The fee can be paid on a per year basis. The fee can change depending on whether the student chooses hotel accommodation or not.

Students opting for a scholarship might also pay less fee, and if the students are taking admission under a quota, the fee can also vary. Please keep in mind that the institute can decide to increase or decrease the fee at their discretion.

If you are looking for more information about the MBA course at Acharya Institute, please browse through https://www.acharya.ac.in/ait/mba.html. You can also send an email at admissions@acharya.ac.in or call +917406644449 to get more information about the admission process.
