How is Acharya Institute of Technology for CSE

June 23, 2021

B.E in Computer Science and Engineering at Acharya Institute of Technology is a well-sought out course for students that want to further their career in this stream. Many students visit the campus from all over to seek admission in CSE courses. So, how is the Acharya Institute of Technology for CSE? Let’s take a look at the factors that make this institute a fantastic choice for CSE.

The CSE Department at Acharya Institute of Technology

The department was established in the year 2000, and since then, the institute has remained at the cutting edge of technology and the subjects taught in the course. The impact of computer engineering is massive on modern life, and the department trains the students about topics like IoT, Cloud computing, Machine learning, Cryptocurrency, and much more.

The course prepares students by honing their problem-solving skills and teaching them about the latest developments in computing. The department also collaborates with the leading industry partners to offer their students the best educational and research resources.

The Fees for CSE at Acharya Institute of Technology

This is a four-year course with a total fee of 2.61 lakh INR. Please keep in mind that the full fee can change as per the institution’s rules, and you should check with them before making your mind up about taking admission to this course.

Placements for CSE at Acharya Institute of Technology

According to reviews left by previous students, nearly 85% of the students in the CSE course get placed. The highest package received by the students was up to 9 LPA. Companies like Infosys, TCS, and several other MNCs visit the campus to recruit students.

CSE Faculty at Acharya Institute of Technology

The faculty at the institute is excellent in teaching and preparing the students for jobs in the industry. The faculty is supportive towards students, and they also make sure that students have an in-depth understanding of the subject. The attendance is strict, and students are expected to attend classes regularly and also turn in their assignments and projects on time.
