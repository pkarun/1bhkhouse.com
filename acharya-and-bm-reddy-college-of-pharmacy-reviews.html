Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy Reviews

April 7, 2021

Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy offer several courses for students interested in pharmacy.

Interested students can choose from M.Pharma, B.Pharma, D.Pharma, and more if they are looking to study pharmacy at this institute.

The college was established in 1992, and it has won several awards for excellence in teaching through the years. The institute received the honor of being the best industry-linked pharmacy college in India by AICTE.

The college was also awarded the SIRO status. The college is the top choice of students in India and international students as well.

Students often write their reviews about the education and study environment for Acharya and BM Reddy College of Pharmacy; let’s look at some of these reviews with the students’ standard points.

The college is known for adherence to the curriculum, and the attendance is strict. The faculty of the college is friendly and highly experienced in teaching their chosen subjects. The college has a well-equipped library and a high-speed WiFi connection so the students can access educational resources with ease.

Students here have ample scope for studying their subjects from various sources as per their needs and requirements.

Students also mentioned in their reviews that the college organizes industrial visits to get familiarized with the industry practices in their chosen discipline.

There are regular tests, and students are assessed based on their performance in the tests.

The college also gets visits from companies like Medchem and Mylan for placements of final year students.

Many international companies also visit the pharmacy department to recruit talent from the students of the college.
