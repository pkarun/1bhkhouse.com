Acharya College Engineering Placements

April 7, 2021

Acharya Institute of Technology offers some of the most sought-after courses for students interested in the field of engineering. The engineering placements from Acharya College make this institute a lucrative choice for students who are looking for a powerful beginning of their career.

The institute takes care of the students’ placement needs by offering them guidance and training in their placement cell.

The placement cell conducts every step of the recruitment process, from mock interviews to group discussion sessions, so that students have a good idea of performing in the actual recruitment interview.

Through the years, the Acharya Institute has placed 100% of its students in some of the most well-known industry houses. Final-year students can also get placement through campus recruitment. Pre-final year students can enjoy internship opportunities that can understand the industry and a clear view of the stream they want to choose.

Companies Visiting Acharya Institute of Technology for Placements

As Bangalore is India’s IT hub, the Acharya Institute of Technology recruitment gets visits from companies like TCS, Accenture, Godrej, Kotak, Voltas, IndiaMart, Mahindra, Infosys, Wipro, and more.

Engineering Placements at Acharya Institute of Technology in 2019

In 2019, more than 35 companies visited AIT, and 194 students were placed. The lead recruitment company was Infosys. The average package for recruitment was 3 LPA, and the highest package was 21 LPA.

If you are interested in learning more, you can check out Acharya Institute of Technology at www.acharya.ac.in.
