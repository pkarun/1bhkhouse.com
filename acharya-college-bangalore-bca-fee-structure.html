Acharya College Bangalore BCA Fee Structure

May 23, 2021

The BCA program at Acharya College Bangalore is a popular stream for students that are interested in computer applications and related fields. Many students are interested in knowing the Acharya College Bangalore, BCA fee structure so that they can see if the course is within their budget. The Acharya Institute of Technology BCA fees is 56,000 annually. For the three year course, this fee comes up to 1,68,000 INR.

Students can pay this fee on an annual basis. In case any students choose to take hostel admission along with the BCA course, the charges will be added to the fee.

The eligibility criteria for Acharya Institute BCA admission is that the student should have passed Class 12th with a minimum of 45% marks. Candidates also need to have studied Mathematics as one of the subjects in the 12th class. The total marks scored by the candidates will be considered for admission into the course.

You can find out more about the BCA program, the curriculum, the course outcomes, and more by browsing through https://www.acharya.ac.in/aigs/bca.html. You can also call +917406644449 or send an email to admissions@acharya.ac.in for more information about the BCA course or the institute.
