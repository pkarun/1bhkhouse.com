Acharya Institute of Technology MBA Cut off

May 23, 2021

An admission in the prestigious Acharya Institute of Technology MBA course depends on the cut off for the year. The cut off number is different every year depending on the overall result of the exams and the academic performance of the students seeking admission in the college.

If you are looking for the cut off for this session, you can get the information from the official channels such as the college website at www.acharya.ac.in.

For admission in the MBA course, students need to give the KMAT test. The test is open to students all over India and the registrations will likely start from July 5th, 2021.

The KMAT exam will be held in the third week of August, but the official date can change. Based on the result of the exam, the cut off for the next session of MBA in Acharya Institute of Technology will be announced.
