Acharya College BBA Fee Structure

May 23, 2021

Acharya Institute of Technology offers BBA for students interested in business administration and MBA for further studies. The Department of Management at Acharya Institute of Technology was started in 2007. The department is affiliated with Bangalore City University. With the help of learned and qualified teaching faculty, the staff offers students a new way of looking at curriculum and their subject matter.

Students and parents are often looking for Acharya Institute of Technology’s BBA fee structure. The yearly fee for the course is 60,000 INR, and the three-year course with the added charges comes up to 2,50,000 INR. The latest fee can be different than the one given here and the college administration can offer a better estimate of the latest fee amount.

The course duration is three years, and candidates need to have two years of pre-university course of Karnataka State or its equivalent for admission eligibility. The institute can also change eligibility conditions, and interested candidates should get in touch with the college administration to get a better idea of the latest guidelines.

You can find out more about the course by browsing through https://www.acharya.ac.in/aigs/bba.html. To get information about admission in the BBA course at Acharya Institute, send an email at admissions@acharya.ac.in or call +917406644449.
