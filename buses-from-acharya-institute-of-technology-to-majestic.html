Buses from Acharya Institute of Technology to Majestic Bus Station

April 7, 2021

Traveling from Acharya Institute of Technology to the Majestic Bus Station is easy as you can easily find several buses to reach the location. The distance between the two locations is 18.7 km, and it takes nearly 1 hour and 30 minutes to cover this distance, depending on the traffic.  The bus ticket costs 32 rupees. You might need to walk a short distance on both ends of the journey.

Which Buses to Choose for Going from Acharya Institute of Technology to Majestic Bus station?

You can choose the buses 251, 251M, 253J, 254A, 266D, 90E, 99B, 275C, 276C and others. The buses are available throughout the day, and you can get a bus every 20 minutes.
