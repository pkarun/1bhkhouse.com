Acharya Polytechnic College, Bangalore Fee Structure

April 6, 2021

Acharya Polytechnic College is one of the most well-recognized names in the region for a variety of courses. The Acharya Polytechnic College was established in 1990 and the college offers over 11 courses in the engineering stream.

The college offers three-year diplomas for the courses that are spread across various streams of engineering and non-engineering fields.

Fee for Three Year Diploma at Acharya Polytechnic, Bangalore

The course fee is one of the first queries of students interested in getting a diploma from Acharya Polytechnic college.

The 3-year diploma programs offer specialization in fields ranging from engineering and non-engineering to technical. As mentioned, there are over 11 courses offered, and the fees for each can vary from Rs. 1.25 lakhs to Rs. 1.85 lakhs depending on the course. The fees are paid on a per-year basis.

Students interested in the courses can get in touch with the administration of Acharya Polytechnic College by sending them an email or giving them a call. You can also find more details by visiting the Acharya Polytechnic College website.
