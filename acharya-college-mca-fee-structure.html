Acharya College MCA Fee Structure

May 23, 2021

Acharya College is a well-known institute for higher education in Bangalore. Acharya Institute of Technology offers MCA along with several other courses for students that are interested in higher studies.

The quality of education and the teaching ability of professors at Acharya Institute of Technology leaves no stone unturned in helping students reach their maximum potential.

Students often want to know the Acharya Institute of Technology MCA fee structure when they seek admission in this course.

This is a two year course and the fee per year is 91,900 INR. Interested candidates should keep in mind that the total amount of fee can change depending on the additional expenses or any other charges added by the institute. Students should call the college and confirm the fee before making their mind up about the course.

The eligibility for MCA at Acharya Institute of Technology is Bachelor’s Degree in any discipline from a recognized university.

Students also need to have had mathematics as a subject at 10+2 or graduation level. The applicants will be chosen on the base of their MAT / GMAT / KMAT / PGCET scores.

You can learn more about the course by checking the details at this link https://www.acharya.ac.in/ait/mca.html. To find out fee details for MCA at Acharya College, send an email to admissions@acharya.ac.in or call +917406644449.
