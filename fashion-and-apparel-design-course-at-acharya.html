Fashion and Apparel Design Course at Acharya College Bangalore

April 7, 2021

Acharya College Bangalore offers a B.Sc. in Fashion and Apparel Design to students interested in pursuing a career in this field. 

The Department of Fashion and Apparel Design was started by Acharya Institutes in 2005. The institute can teach 60 students in each batch, and the FAD program is affiliated with Bangalore University. The duration of this course is three years. This is a full-time course.

Eligibility for B.Sc in Fashion Design at Acharya College Bangalore

If you plan to study B.Sc. in Fashion and Apparel Design at Acharya College, you need to have passed your pre-university higher education, 10+2, or ‘A’ level from a recognized board in any discipline. Candidates who have studied for any 3-year Diploma in Karnataka in any commercial practice can straight away get admission in 2nd year (3 sem).

Details of the Fashion and Apparel Design Program

The program considers the needs and requirements of the modern fashion industry to prepare the students to fulfill these demands.

The course covers various fashion and apparel design areas, ranging from software to design patterns to understanding the fashion industry and changing trends. Along with providing practical knowledge, the course also teaches students about the fashion industry’s various demands in different areas such as fashion photography, fashion retailing, textile testing methods, and much more.

The Department of Fashion Design also organizes a Portfolio presentation and an academic Fashion show called VASTRA that helps budding fashion designers get exposure to the industry. You can find out more about the course by browsing through https://www.acharya.ac.in/aigs/bsc-fashion.html.

Fee Structure for Fashion and Apparel Design Program at Acharya College

The fee for the complete three year program can come to nearly 1.65 lakh INR. In case you also choose hostel accommodation, the fee for three years can come up to 5.09 lakh INR. Students can pay the fee on a per year basis. Further queries about the overall fee structure for three years should be addressed to the college administration.

You can learn more about Acharya Institutes Bangalore by visiting www.acharya.ac.in. Students can also get in touch with the administration by calling +917406644449 or by sending an email to admissions@acharya.ac.in.
