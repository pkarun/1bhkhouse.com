Hostels at Acharya Institute of Technology, Bangalore – Everything You Need to Know

April 6, 2021

Students looking for a hostel while studying in Acharya College, Bangalore, can find affordable hostels and facilities. Acharya Halls of Residence offers a complete range of facilities for residents ranging from dining facilities, laundry services, common areas, and much more.

The hostel residents also have access to food services within the campus to enjoy affordable meals such as lunch or snacks. The Acharya Institute of Technology provides a fantastic learning experience by encouraging students to participate in committees and events to feel at home while in the hostel.

Acharya College Hostel Reviews

College hostel reviews mention the overall comfort and ease of stay at the Acharya College hostels. The availability of fast WiFi and spacious rooms adds to the overall quality of stay at the hostel.

The rooms are also spacious so that students can study as well as relax at ease. The food available in a mess offers delicious and hygienic cuisine that also changes regularly so that the students do not get bored of eating the same food.

Hostels and Facilities for Acharya Institute of Technology

There are twelve residences from which five are for boys and seven are for girls. The hostel has a capacity of 1500 students. There are single, double, and three bedded rooms available for students. The on-campus facilities help the students in making the most of their time by devoting it to studies.

The hostels are equipped with all the facilities to help students live a comfortable life and focus on their studies. There are services like laundry, high-speed WiFi, room services, elevator, 24/7 guard, CCTV monitoring, and more.

The students are expected to adhere to the curfew timings when staying in the hostel. The rooms have all the necessary infrastructure such as a bed, table, chair, cupboard, curtains, and more. There are libraries in every wing of the college, and there is also the main library where students can explore books on a wide range of topics.

Dining Arrangements for Hostel Students at Acharya Institute of Technology

The hostel has hygienic cooking arrangements for the students, and the menu has vegetarian and non-vegetarian food. The mess menu is decided every month by the Mess Committee that comprises Hostel Authority, Mess Manager, and student representatives.

For international students, kitchen facilities are provided in the international student hostel to cook for themselves.

Sports and Games at Acharya Institute of Technology

The hostel also has facilities for sports and games such as volleyball, basketball, and indoor games. Students can use the sports facilities that include horse riding as well.

Acharya Institute of Technology Hostel Fees

The hostel fees at Acharya Institute of Technology varies from 1 to 1.5 lakh per year, depending on the facilities you choose. The fee tier will change for students that select their hostel stay with or without food.

You can learn more about the Acharya Institute, Bangalore, by visiting https://www.acharya.ac.in/. To find out more about the hostel in Acharya College, Bangalore, please visit https://www.acharya.ac.in/extra/campuslife/hostel.html.
