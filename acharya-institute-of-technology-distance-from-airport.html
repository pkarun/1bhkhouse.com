Acharya Institute of Technology Distance from Airport

April 7, 2021

Are you planning to study at Acharya Institute of Technology? If you are visiting the campus for the first time, you might want to find out the Acharya Institute of Technology distance from the airport.

The distance from Acharya Institute of Technology to Kempegowda International Airport Bengaluru is 41.4 kilometers. The travel time on this route is 1 hour and 4 minutes, depending on the traffic and road conditions. If you plan to visit the airport from the college, it is advised to leave early so that you do not get delayed by traffic.

Cab Services from Acharya Institute of Technology to Airport

You can also choose cab services to get from the airport to Acharya Institute of Technology.

Ola/Uber can cost you in the range of rupees 800-1000 for one-way travel. The prices can increase depending on the time of the day and availability of the cabs.

You can find out more details about the route by clicking the Google Maps link https://goo.gl/maps/DZPBCp3EokQKBYzu8

How to Reach Acharya Institute of Technology from the Bangalore Airport by Bus

If you plan to take a bus from Bangalore airport to Acharya Institute, you can take a bus to Mekhri Circle. From there, you can take 253C/250J/250W and get down at Janapriya Apartments. The bus 250Za will take you to the Campus from there.

The travel time for a distance of 39 kilometers can be up to three hours depending on the traffic, and the bus tickets can cost around 100 rupees. Keep in mind that this mode of travel might require you to walk between bus stations as well, so travel by bus only if you cannot travel by taxi or your own vehicle.

Here’s a link to the Google Map route for the bus travel: https://goo.gl/maps/BJLRMi7xEQ1u5uKD6

Conclusion

Whether you are coming to the Acharya Institute of Technology from the airport or going to the airport from the institute, it is always a good idea to choose a private vehicle as it will save your time. The bus timings can lead to wastage of time as the bus will stop at several locations before reaching your destination.
