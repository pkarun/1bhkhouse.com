Acharya Institute of Technology Engineering Fee Structure

April 7, 2021

Acharya Institute of Technology offers a wide range of engineering courses that cater to the needs and requirements of students from all over. If you’re interested in studying engineering at Acharya Institute of Technology, you would surely want to know about the fee structure for engineering courses.

Let’s take a quick look at various engineering courses available here and how much each of them will cost you.

Please keep in mind that the information provided here is correct at the time of writing of this article but the fee amounts can change in future. The fee can also vary depending on whether you choose hostel accommodation or not. It’s best to contact the AIT administration to find out the latest fee structure for engineering courses.

Bachelor of Engineering Fee Structure – Acharya Institute of Technology

There are several courses available under the Bachelor of Engineering stream. The fee for these courses ranges from 1.85 lacs to 5.95 lacs annually. Each course is for four years and the study mode is regular.
