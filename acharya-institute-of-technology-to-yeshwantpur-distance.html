Distance Between Acharya Institute of Technology to Yeshwantpur

April 6, 2021

If you are studying at Acharya Institute of Technology, you might need to commute to and from Yeshwantpur every now and then. It is important to know about the distance between the two locations and how much time it takes to commute between the two places.

Route Option 1: From Acharya Institute of Technology to Yeshwantpur

According to Google Maps, the distance between the two places is 14.4 kilometers if you go through NH75. It takes 32 minutes to travel this distance if you are traveling by a car. Here are the directions according to Google Maps

BMTC Bus Route for Acharya Institute of Technology to Yeshwantpur

If you choose to take the bus, the distance is 15 kilometers and takes nearly one hour depending on the traffic and the availability of the bus.

According to the bus schedule, the buses leave every twenty minutes from Kempegowda Bus Station near Soladevanahalli Police Station that is 950m away from Acharya Institute of Technology. The bus ticket costs 27 Rs, and you can also call BMTC at 18004251663 for more information.
