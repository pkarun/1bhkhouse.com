Acharya Institute of Technology Highest Package

June 23, 2021

Acharya Institute of Technology is located in Bangalore, and it is one of the premier institutes for higher education in the region. There are many courses on offer here, from MBA to engineering and biotech and more. Interested students always want to know the highest package that job seekers received when they were placed through the Acharya Institute of Technology.

The answer can vary depending on a range of factors. Several companies visit the campus looking for new talent that will suit the job requirements in their company. While one company might offer a great package, the other will offer a package according to their budget. The package also depends on the caliber of the students, performance in the interview, and the position for which the student is giving the interview.

Highest Packages Offered at Acharya Institute of Technology

Here’s a quick look at some of the highest packages offered to students through campus placements. The students from previous years mention that they have received offers of the highest packages being 20 LPA in the stream of Information Science and Engineering. The average package offered at the Acharya Institute of Technology was 12 LPA.

For Computer Science and Engineering, the highest package offered was 9 LPA and the lowest package offered was 4.5 LPA. Companies like Wipro, Microsoft, Infosys, and other multinational companies visit the campus to recruit talent from here as well.

For Mechanical Engineering, the highest package offered was 14 LPA and the lowest package on offer was 2.4 LPA. The Biotechnology stream on the other had received the highest package at 9 LPA.
